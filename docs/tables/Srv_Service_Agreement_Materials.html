    Table Srv_Service_Agreement_Materials

    Entity

    Entity: Applications.Service.ServiceAgreementMaterials

    Contains the free materials, included in the service agreement. Entity: Srv_Service_Agreement_Materials

    Owner Tables Hierarchy

    • Srv_Service_Agreements
    • Gen_Documents

    Summary

    Name Type Description
    End_Date datetime End date to which the agreedment for the material is valid. For the agreement period, the material could be used free of charge in service activities.
    Line_No int Consecutive line number, unique within the document. Usually is increasing in steps of 10, like in 10, 20, 30, etc.
    Product_Id uniqueidentifier Paid or agreed in advance material that won't be invoiced after service activities
    Quantity decimal(18, 3) Quantity of the agreed material
    Quantity_Base decimal(18, 3) Readonly The equivalence of Quantity in the base measurement category of the product.
    Quantity_Unit_Id uniqueidentifier The measurement unit of Quantity.
    Row_Version timestamp
    Service_Agreement_Id uniqueidentifier
    Service_Agreement_Material_Id uniqueidentifier PK
    Standard_Quantity_Base decimal(18, 3) Readonly The theoretical quantity in base measurement unit according to the current measurement dimensions for the product. Used to measure the execution.
    Start_Date datetime Start date from which the agreedment for the material is valid. For the agreement period, the material could be used free of charge in service activities.
