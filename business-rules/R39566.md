# R39566 WarehouseReconciliationLine - Location Zone
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Code**             | R39566                                                                                        |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Entity**           | @Logistics.Wms.WarehouseReconciliationLines                                                   |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Name**             | LocationZonе                                                                            |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Attribute**        | WarehouseZone, WarehouseLocation                                                              |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Layer**            | Back-End                                                                                      |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Events**           | Commit                                                                                        |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Priority**         | Normal                                                                                        |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Modify**           | No                                                                                            |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Applicable         | [ALL // no condition needed                                                                   |
| Legislations**       | ](xref:applicable-legislations)                                                               |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Action**           | When both Warehouse Location and Warehouse Zone are filled in the line, check that the        |
|                      | selected Warehouse Zone is the same as the location’s zone, or is a parent zone of it         |
|                      | (directly or through one or more parents)                                                     |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Description**      | The rule validates that the lines does not contain Locations from another Zone,               | 
|                      | i.e. the selected Warehouse Zone must match the Location’s Zone or a parent Zone of it.       |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Message**          | The Warehouse Location in line {LineNo} is from a different Warehouse Zone. Please check the  |
|                      | Warehouse Zone and try again.                                                                 |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Version**          | Introduced:                                                                                   |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Revocable**        | NO                                                                                            |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
