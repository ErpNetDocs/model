# R101675-2 Bundle - Activation Date After Deactivation Date Not Allowed
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Code**             | R101675-2                                                                                        |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Entity**           | @Crm.Pos.Bundles                                                                                 |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Name**             | ActivationDateAfterDeactivationDateNotAllowed                                                    |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Attribute**        | ActivationDate, DeactivationDate                                                                 |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Layer**            | Front-End                                                                                        |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Events**           | AttributeChanged(ActivationDate, DeactivationDate)                                               |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Priority**         | Normal                                                                                           |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Modify**           | NO                                                                                               |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Action**           | When changing ActivationDate or DeactivationDate - if both are filled and                        |
|                      | ActivationDate is later than DeactivationDate, throw an error.                                   |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Description**      | Prevents a bundle from being configured with an activation date that is later than its           |
|                      | deactivation date, which would result in a bundle that is never active. The check is             |
|                      | skipped when either date is empty.                                                               |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Message**          | The activation date cannot be after the deactivation date.                                       |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Version**          | Introduced: v.27.1                                                                               |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Revocable**        | YES                                                                                              |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
