# R39256-1 Sequence Selector - Sales And Refunds Require POS Device
+------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Code**         | R39256-1                                                                                       |
+------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Entity**       | @Crm.Pos.SequenceSelectors                                                                     |
+------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Name**         | SalesAndRefundsRequirePosDevice                                                                |
+------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Attribute**    | PosDevice                                                                                      |
+------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Layer**        | Back-End                                                                                       |
+------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Events**       | Commit                                                                                         |
+------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Priority**     | Normal                                                                                         |
+------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Modify**       | NO                                                                                             |
+------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Action**       | If (SaleKind == NormalSale OR SaleKind == ReturnOrRefund):                                     |
|                  |    Validate that (PosDevice != NULL)                                                           |
+------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Description**  | Ensures that a fiscal POS Device is assigned for transactions that require fiscal              |
|                  | registration. The rule validates that for "Normal Sale" and "Return or Refund" sale kinds,     |
|                  | the PosDevice field is not empty. Other sale kinds are allowed to remain without a device.     |
|                  |                                                                                                |
+------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Message**      | A POS device is required for normal sales and refunds.                                         |
+------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Version**      | Introduced: v.26.2                                                                             |
+------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Revocable**    | NO                                                                                             |
+------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
