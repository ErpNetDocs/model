# R39658 AccountingVoucher - Debit Equals Debit Reporting And Credit Equals Credit Reporting
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Code**             | R39658                                                                                        |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Entity**           | @Finance.Accounting.AccountingVouchers                                                        |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Name**             | DebitEqualsDebitReportingAndCreditEqualsCreditReporting                                       |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Attribute**        | AccountingVoucherLines.Debit, AccountingVoucherLines.DebitReporting,AccountingVoucherLines.Credit, AccountingVoucherLines.CreditReporting|
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Layer**            | Back-End                                                                                      |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Events**           | Releasing +                                                                                   |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Priority**         | Normal                                                                                        |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Modify**           | NO                                                                                            |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Action**           | If AccountingVoucherLine.Currency = EnterpriseCompany.ReportingCurrency <br>                  |
|                      | and DocumentDate <= EnterpriseCompany.ReportingCurrencyEndDate <br>                           |
|                      | OR EnterpriseCompany.ReportingCurrencyEndDate Is Null <br>                                    |
|                      | for each voucher line validate that <br>                                                      |
|                      | Debit = DebitReporting and  Credit = CreditReporting                                          |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Description**      | When the document date is before the Reporting Currency End Date and the currency of the voucher line <br>|
|                      | is the reporting currency, validates that the Debit amount is equal to the Debit Reporting amount <br>|
|                      | and the Credit amount is equal to the Credit Reporting amount.                                |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Message**          | In line number {LineNo} of document {DocumentText} there is difference between the amounts in Debit <br>|
|                      | and Debit RC or Credit and Credit RC.<br><br>                                                 |
|                      | When the reporting currency is selected as a currency of a particular accounting voucher line,|
|                      | then Debit must equal Debit RC and Credit must equal Credit RC.                               |
|                      | *Sort Messages By Attribute: LineNo*                                                          |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Version**          | Introduced:                                                                                   |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Revocable**        | YES                                                                                           |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
