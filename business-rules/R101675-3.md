# R101675-3 Bundle Line - Quantity Must Be Greater Than Zero
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Code**             | R101675-3                                                                                        |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Entity**           | @Crm.Pos.BundleLines                                                                             |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Name**             | QuantityMustBeGreaterThanZero                                                                    |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Attribute**        | Quantity                                                                                         |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Layer**            | Front-End                                                                                        |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Events**           | AttributeChanged(Quantity)                                                                       |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Priority**         | Normal                                                                                           |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Modify**           | NO                                                                                               |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Action**           | When changing Quantity - if the entered value is not greater than zero, throw an error.          |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Description**      | Ensures that each bundle line has a positive quantity, so a bundle cannot contain a line         |
|                      | with a zero or negative quantity.                                                                |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Message**          | The quantity must be greater than zero.                                                          |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Version**          | Introduced: v.27.1                                                                               |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Revocable**        | YES                                                                                              |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
