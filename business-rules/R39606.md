# R39606 IntrastatDeclaration - Declaration And Enterprise Company Same Base Currency
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Code**                    | R39606                                                                                |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Entity**                  | @Regulatory.Intrastat.Declarations                                                    |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Name**                    | DeclarationAndEnterpriseCompanySameBaseCurrency                                       |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Attribute**               | BaseCurrency                                                                          |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Layer**                   | Back-End                                                                              |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Events**                  | Commit                                                                                |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Priority**                | Normal                                                                                |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Modify**                  | NO                                                                                    |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Applicable Legislations** | [ALL // no condition                                                                  |
