# R101675-4 Bundle - Activation Date Not After Deactivation Date
+------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Code**         | R101675-4                                                                                        |
+------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Entity**       | @Crm.Pos.Bundles                                                                                 |
+------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Name**         | ActivationDateNotAfterDeactivationDate                                                           |
+------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Attribute**    | ActivationDate, DeactivationDate                                                                 |
+------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Layer**        | Back-End                                                                                         |
+------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Events**       | Commit                                                                                           |
+------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Priority**     | Normal                                                                                           |
+------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Modify**       | NO                                                                                               |
+------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Action**       | If (ActivationDate != NULL AND DeactivationDate != NULL):                                        |
|                  |    Validate that (ActivationDate <= DeactivationDate)                                            |
+------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Description**  | Server-side counterpart of the front-end check. Guarantees, regardless of the entry              |
|                  | channel (UI or API), that a bundle is never saved with an activation date later than its         |
|                  | deactivation date. The check is skipped when either date is empty.                               |
+------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Message**      | The activation date cannot be after the deactivation date.                                       |
+------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Version**      | Introduced: v.27.1                                                                               |
+------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Revocable**    | NO                                                                                               |
+------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
