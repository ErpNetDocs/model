# Table Wms_Warehouse_Reconciliations


## Entity

Entity: [Logistics.Wms.WarehouseReconciliations](~/entities/Logistics.Wms.WarehouseReconciliations.md)

Contains plan for reconciliation of physical stock in the warehouse, initiating and managing warehouse counting by generating corresponding Warehouse Orders. Entity: Wms_Warehouse_Reconciliations (Introduced in version 26.1.3.87)
